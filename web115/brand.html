Brand Guide

Concept

The purpose behind Jeffers Racing and Performance is to provide consultation services for those who wish to reach their full potential on track day. From car setup, engineering, driver coaching and making sure everyone is up to date on news and upcoming events. We are here to help in any way to get more people on track and having fun in the hobby we love.

Fonts and Colors

  • Fonts - My header will use a font called "Radium Corp.". All other text will be in "Futura".
  • Background - My page background will utilize a carbon fiber weave design.
  • Colors - My font color will be an off white color, #E8E4C9. This will be over a dark grey sectional background in some cases, #18191A. Link hover color will be a dark red, #A40000.
